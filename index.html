Subsonic, on your Mac

Submariner provides the Mac way to access self-hosted music and podcasts with Subsonic and compatible alternatives. Fully native, no Electron, with the polish you'd expect.

Submariner development has resumed after a long hiatus. Version 2.0 has been released on 2022-10-25.
